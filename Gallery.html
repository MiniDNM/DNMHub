Latest Advancements in CPU

Intel Core i9-14900K - The Intel Core i9-14900K is a desktop processor with 24 cores, launched in October 2023, at an MSRP of $589. It is part of the Core i9 lineup, using the Raptor Lake Refresh architecture with Socket 1700.

Latest Advancements in GPU

NVIDIA RTX 5000 Ada Generation - The NVIDIA RTX 5000 Ada Generation is a professional graphics card by NVIDIA, launched on August 9th, 2023. Built on the 5 nm process, and based on the AD102 graphics processor.

Latest Advancements in SSD

Solidigm P5430 31 TB - The Solidigm P5430 is a solid-state drive in the U.2 form factor, launched on May 16th, 2023. It is only available in the 31 TB capacity.
